## Final Step: Deploy to Vercel

1.  Log in to [Vercel](https://vercel.com/) (sign in with GitHub).
2.  On your dashboard, click **Add New...** -> **Project**.
3.  In the **Import Git Repository** section, find **`urbanrise_interiors_347`** and click **Import**.
4.  **Configure Project**:
    *   **Framework Preset**: Vercel will automatically detect it.
    *   **Root Directory**: Leave as `./`.
5.  Click **Deploy**.

Vercel will build your site and give you a live URL (e.g., `urbanrise-interiors-347.vercel.app`).
